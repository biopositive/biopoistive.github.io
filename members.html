Dr. Soumya Pandit

Principal Investigator / Lab Incharge

Area of Interest: Microbial electrochemical cell biofilm and biofouling; biohythane; nanomaterial synthesis and characterization; microalgal biomass production; bioenergy and biofuel.

Description

Dr. Soumya Pandit is an associate professor and heads the Bio-Positive Lab at Sharda University in Greater Noida, Delhi NRC, India. His expertise includes Bioprocess Engineering, Industrial Biotechnology, Environmental Microbiology, and Waste Management. He earned his doctorate from the Department of Biotechnology at the Indian Institute of Technology, Kharagpur, and completed postdoctoral research at The Zuckerberg Institute for Water Research (ZIWR), Ben-Gurion University of the Negev, Israel. He has received post-doctoral fellowships from the Planning and Budget Commission of Israel and North West University, South Africa. Dr. Pandit has a strong publication record in international conferences, particularly in environmental biotechnology and bioenergy, with an H-Index of 24.
